Minorities X S. T. E. M



We are STEM (WSTEM) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit, led by students and professionals of color. Our goal is to bridge the gap of between NYC’s minorities and the STEM fields. This includes, but is not limited to; inspiring NYC’s younger generation of minorities to pursue STEM letting them explore the various aspects and subjects within the sciences. As well as, networking through STEM fields with both students and professionals of color in order to obtain internship or job offers and witness how people of color play a large role in STEM. We also strongly believe, Enhancing minorities’ critical skills and technical skills, that are not taught at every institution are vital for professional work and growth such as Python, MATLAB, Excel, etc.

Our History

WSTEM was founded in March of 2019 by Rua Hamid while she was only 21 years of age while an undergrad in City University Queens College. Being the only student of color as a Geology major, she faced and noticed many disparparites against her in the field. As a result, she assembled a group of students of color in NYC that shared an interest in the sciences and created WSTEM. In 2020 everything became official, and WSTEM became a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. WSTEM is an obligation to demonstrate and prove that WE, people of color, are needed and belong in the sciences and will not be held back further due to our current social, political and economic environment.
