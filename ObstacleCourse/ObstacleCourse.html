Obstacle Course

Instructions

  • The objective of this game is to avoid the square making contact with walls by that appear on the screen from the right.
  • You can use the up, down, back, and forward keys to avoid the obstructions
  • As time goes on the speed of your object increases, and this makes it harder for you to avoid the blocks.

Scoring

  • Your current score will be dislpayed on the top of screenr
  • Your score increments by 1 for each millisecond that goes by
  • The longer you avoid the obstructions the higher your score
  • When you lose, you viewport will freeze, and your score will be displayed in red.
