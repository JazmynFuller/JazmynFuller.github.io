Our Vision

Compelled by the words of Christ, in partnership with other Churches and followers of Christ. La Jornada envisions changed lives by providing to the spiritual, physical, and emotional needs of the immigrant and poor communities of Queens.

Our purpose

Our Purpose

To Evangelize

By using servant evangelism, Joyfully sharing the good news of the sovereign love of God, and calling people to repentance, to personal faith in Jesus Christ as Savior and Lord,to active membership in the church, and to obedient service in the world.  

To Educate

By creating a process of imparting knowledge, skill and judgment, so as to develop the spiritual, intellectual and moral, faculties of the Immigrant community, to help the in their integretion into American society.

To Empower

By changing existing power relations and the forces that marginalizes the Immigrant community. We will crate a process to help them developed their abilities to shape their life's according to their own aspirations and achieve their full potential as members of the American society.

To Employ

By becoming a referral service, our goal is to help facilitate productive work experiences for employers and employees alike. We will train the employees  to do the best quality job . We will expect from the employers to pay fair wages and humane treatment.. We do not accept fees or ask questions.

What we do? 

Food Preparation and Street Delivery


Once a month we come together to make sandwiches and gallons of hot chocolate to give to the people looking for work (daylaborers) in the streets of Queens New York City . We make over 300 meals. This is a great time for fellowship and usually takes just a couple of hours. We always finish with a prayer.
